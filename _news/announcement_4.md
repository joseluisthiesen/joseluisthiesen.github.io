I am looking for motivated **Ph.D. students** to join my research group in computational mechanics and biomechanics at UFSC. This position is associated with the official POSMEC selection process (Edital nº 11/2025) and is open to candidates holding a degree in Engineering, Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, or related fields.

**Scholarship:** R$ 3.100,00/month (CAPES/CNPq — full dedication required)

**Research topics of interest:**
- Multiscale and second-order computational homogenization
- Poromechanics and biomechanics of soft biological tissues
- Micropolar (Cosserat) continuum mechanics
- Orthopedic biomechanics and finite element analysis of implants

**Rolling admissions:** the program accepts applications year-round through multiple windows. The current open window closes **July 10, 2026**.

**How to apply:**
Applications are submitted through the POSMEC online system at [capg.sistemas.ufsc.br/inscricao](https://capg.sistemas.ufsc.br/inscricao/). A research plan (6–10 pages) and a letter of acceptance from an advisor are required — please contact me before applying.
