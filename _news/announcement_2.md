I am glad to be part of the organizing committee of the **IX National Meeting of Biomechanical Engineering (ENEBI 2026)**, to be held on **October 7–9, 2026**, at UFSC, Florianópolis, Brazil.

ENEBI is the main Brazilian forum for researchers and professionals working at the intersection of engineering and the life sciences, covering topics across computational biomechanics, experimental biomechanics, biomedical devices, and more.

**Important dates:**

| Milestone | Date |
|---|---|
| Abstract submission deadline | May 11, 2026 |
| Acceptance notification | May 18, 2026 |
| Full paper submission deadline | June 29, 2026 |
| Conference | October 7–9, 2026 |

We look forward to welcoming the biomechanics community to Florianópolis! For more information, visit the [official event page](https://eventos.abcm.org.br/enebi2026/).
